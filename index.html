How it works

This tool uses industry-standard CPM (advertiser cost per 1,000 impressions) and RPM (revenue per 1,000 views after platform cuts) figures to give a quick earnings estimate. Values are illustrative — tune rates in data.json for more accuracy.

  • Quick: Get a forecast in seconds.
  • Niche-aware: Values vary by niche such as Finance, Tech, Gaming, etc.
  • Shareable: Use the estimate for planning and pitches.

FAQ

What is CPM vs RPM?

CPM is what advertisers pay per 1,000 ad impressions. RPM is the revenue you receive per 1,000 views after the platform takes its share.

Are the numbers real?

Values in the site are illustrative defaults estimated by Gemini. You can adjust the niche data in data.json to reflect real-world medians for your audience. Consider using conservative and optimistic ranges for planning.
